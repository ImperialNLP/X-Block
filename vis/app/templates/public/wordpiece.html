Multimodal Hate Speech Detection - About Wordpieces

When analyzing text, the first thing we do is to split it up into 'tokens'.

Often these tokens are words and punctuation, but sometimes for rarer or misspelt words, we may split these words up into 'wordpieces' that are just components of these words.

Whenever a token in a text visualization starts with ' - -' it indicates that it was once part of a larger word that has been split up.
